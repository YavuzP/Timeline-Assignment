Mansa Musa

Mansa Musa was the ninth mansa of the Mali Empire, which reached its territorial peak during his reign.


  • Mansa Musa was born during the 13th century.
  • He rose to power in 1312 C.E.
  • Made a pilgrimage to Mecca between 1324 and 1325 spanning 2,700 miles.
  • Travelled with an entourage of tens of thousands of people and dozens of camels, each carrying 300~ pounds of gold.
  • Returned from Mecca in the 1320s
  • Built mosques and large public buildings along with major Islamic universities.
  • Brought architects and scholars from across the Islamic world to his kingdom.
  • Died in 1337 and was succeeded by his sons.
  • The empire fell apart years after his death due to civil wars and assasinations.
